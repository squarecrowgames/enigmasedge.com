Are You Ready to Face the Edge?

Embark on an unforgettable journey in Keystone: Supernova, a 3D roguelike science-fantasy RPG that reimagines classic dungeon crawling. Awakened after a century in cryostasis, you find yourself trapped within the remnants of a crashed spaceship, now a sprawling labyrinth beneath the medieval planet of Delavorn.

Lead a party of three unique adventurers—each with vital skills—through a dark and mysterious maze where advanced technology and ancient magic intertwine. Confront formidable foes in strategic turn-based combat, solve intricate environmental puzzles that reshape your path, and uncover a narrative woven into every corner of this atmospheric world. Every decision matters, every shadow holds a secret—will you face the Edge?

Adventure

Lead your three adventurers, each with their own distinct skills and abilities, through the harsh and unforgiving world of Delavorn. Whether solving puzzles or engaging in combat, you'll need to use their abilities in harmony to survive.

Puzzles

In Keystone Supernova, the world itself is a puzzle. Rotate dungeons, unlock ancient mechanisms, and use the environment to your advantage as you navigate through intricate labyrinths filled with hidden secrets.

Combat

Engage in tactical, turn-based combat where strategy and preparation are key. Face a variety of enemies, each with unique abilities and weaknesses. Your choices in battle will determine your survival.

A Living Dungeon

Keystone: Supernova is a role-playing video game set inside a vast, engineered dungeon that responds to player perception and configuration. Players control a small party of characters, each revealing different structural aspects of the environment, uncovering hidden paths, altered geometry, and overlapping layers of reality. Exploration, puzzle-solving, and tactical encounters are all part of the same interconnected system.

The game centers on interacting with the dungeon’s internal mechanisms to change how the world behaves. By configuring key structures, players influence navigation, access, and outcomes as they move through the environment. This release represents an early playable version of Keystone, available to download and run locally.
